About Us

RETTEN CBO is a grassroots organization founded to empower Samburu communities to overcome socio-economic challenges through education, sustainable livelihoods, and natural resource management. ‘RETTEN’ means tighten your belt to achieve hard goals in the Samburu language, reflecting resilience and determination.

Vision

A self-reliant Samburu community with access to quality education, sustainable livelihoods, and a healthy environment.

Mission

To empower communities through education, economic development, and sustainable resource management.

Thematic Areas

Education & Skills

ECD, girl-child education, adult literacy, and vocational training.

Livelihoods

Farmers’ support, VSLAs, agribusiness, and market linkages.

Climate Action

Resilient crops, reforestation, soil & water conservation, renewable energy.

Membership

Open to all Samburu residents (18+).
